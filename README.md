# Collectibles

---

- Numeros Dinosaur Eggs
- 1 Key per level
- 1 Dodo bird to free from its cage per level

# Goals

---

- Collecting all eggs
- Helping Dodo
- Surviving!

# KeyList

---

- AWSD, arrows - movement
- LCTRL - holding a box
- LSHIFT - dash
- LMOUSE - attack
- E - use lever
- ESC - pause menu
